Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

1. What we collect

When you use SimWorld, we may collect:

  • Account information — email address when you sign up or join the waitlist.
  • Simulation inputs — seed material (press releases, policy drafts, etc.) that you upload to run a simulation.
  • Usage data — page views, feature usage, and crash reports to improve the product.

2. How we use your data

  • To run simulations you request.
  • To send product updates and launch notifications (only if you opted in).
  • To improve SimWorld's accuracy and performance.

3. Simulation data

Your seed material is processed server-side to generate agent simulations. It is not shared with other users, used for training, or sold. Simulation data is deleted 30 days after the simulation completes unless you choose to save it.

4. Third parties

We use minimal third-party services:

  • Cloud hosting — for running simulations securely.
  • Analytics — privacy-respecting, cookieless analytics (no personal data shared).

We do not sell your data. Ever.

5. Cookies

SimWorld uses only essential cookies for authentication. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

6. Your rights

You can request to:

  • Access or export your data.
  • Delete your account and all associated data.
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time.

7. Contact

Questions? Email us at privacy@simworld.app.
